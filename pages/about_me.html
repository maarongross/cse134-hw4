Hi! My name is Matthew Gross!

I'm 22 years old, I was born and raised in Los Angeles, California, and I'm currently attending UC San Diego to study Computer Engineering.
When I'm not grinding through various CS and EE assignments, I like to practice clarinet, rock climb, listen to music, game, and speedcube.

I got started in Computer Engineering because of Minecraft

Well, that's mainly due to my friend getting me into building redstone contraptions in the game.


In case you don't know, redstone is effectively Minecraft's version of a wire, and you can build things ranging from basic circuits to a 16-bit computer.

This led to me having a great interest in computer hardware specifically, and this interest only grew after investing myself in various courses relating to that subject matter at UC San Diego.
